2. What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique was mainly built for people to focus. It involves dividing your work time into 25 minutes work time and 5 minutes break time. We need to finish at least one task in the 25 minutes. After each 25 minutes, there is a 5 minute break. This allows you to focus and relax your mind.
